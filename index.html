Biography

Boyi Liu is currently a first year Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA), under the supervision of Prof. Yongxin Tong. He is also a joint Ph.D student at the School of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), under the supervision of Prof. Zimu Zhou. He received his B.E. in 2023 at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University (BUAA).
